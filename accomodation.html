Accomodations

Experience the luxurious rooms available

JHotel has a varieties of rooms and high class amenities that will suit your needs.

The Presidential suite

  • In-room safe and a mini bar
  • The room is turned down daily
  • The bathroom has a heated floor and is well appointed with a bathtub with a shower
  • Hairdryer, luxurious bathrobes, slippers and deluxe beauty products

The Superior King and Twin Rooms offer luxurious elegance with color-balanced tones and comfortable, contemporary design by Andrew Martin, the award-winning interior design firm. Guests are able to enjoy all the modern comforts and technology expected from a leading 5-star hotel. Whether you are travelling for business or pleasure, the exceptional accommodation and quality of services offered by the Grand Palace Hotel make it an ideal choice for your stay in Riga, Latvia.

The elegant rooms and welcoming atmosphere, together with the attentive service of our staff, ensures that our guests will enjoy their stay at the Grand Palace Hotel, and will be able to look back with great fondness of their fascinating visit to the historic city of Riga. Upon availability it is possible to book a smoking or a non-smoking room with connecting doors to a Business King Room or a Junior Suite Twin Room.

The Deluxe suite

  • The spacious 24sq m/258sq ft Deluxe Rooms offer luxuriously elegant surroundings
  • Comfortable sitting area
  • King-sized bed or twin beds
  • Free high-speed Wi-Fi, providing our guests with state-of-the-art technology
  • The bathroom has a heated floor and is well appointed with a bathtub with a shower

The Deluxe Room provides leisure and business travelers with a luxurious retreat, where they can enjoy the atmosphere of this leading hotel. The interior design by Andrew Martin, the award-winning design firm, presents colour-balanced tones and is the perfect space for work or relaxation at the end of the day.

With the restful atmosphere of these elegantly appointed Deluxe King or Twin rooms, together with the attentive service of our staff, our guests are sure to enjoy a memorable stay and take with them fond memories of their visit to the historic city of Riga.

Upon availability it is possible to book a smoking or a non-smoking room with connecting doors to another Deluxe Room.
