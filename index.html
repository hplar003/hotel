Experience the Luxury

Located on the hills of Puerto Galera, a short distance from the famous Promenade des Anglais,JHotel is one of the hotels in the Puerto Galera able to satisfy the different needs of its guests with comfort and first rate services. It is only several km from Calapan City and one of the most sought after hotels by travelers. The hotel has a large parking area , a real luxury in a seaside like Boracay.

If you are looking for peace and tranquillity after days spent walking amongst the city streets or under the sun, the JHotel offers refreshments in its refined indoor rooms. The lounge bar, open 24 hours a day and the restaurant, available for grand wedding events are all in elegant air conditioned rooms. The restaurant service is also available on the terrace.
